# ReplyRadar — Product Hunt Launch

## Listing Details

**Tagline:** Find customers while you sleep — AI that monitors Reddit for your perfect leads

**Description:**
ReplyRadar scans Reddit in real time for people asking about tools and services in your niche. When it finds a match, it drafts a personalized, authentic reply — and you approve with one click.

I built this because I was tired of spending hours scrolling Reddit looking for people to help. Now my AI does the finding, I do the approving, and the conversations happen while I focus on building.

**How it works:**
1. Add your keywords — "best automation tool", "need sysadmin help", "recommend a SaaS builder"
2. ReplyRadar scans 15+ business subreddits every 2 hours
3. AI drafts contextual, non-spammy replies
4. You approve, edit, or reject in 30 seconds each
5. Conversations turn into leads

**Pricing:**
- Starter: $29/mo — 50 keywords, Reddit only
- Pro: $79/mo — 200 keywords, Reddit + X
- Enterprise: $199/mo — unlimited, all platforms, API

**Launch offer:** First 50 users get Pro for $29/mo (lifetime pricing)
